## Trade Rules
- Manual buy orders pay the full cost upfront and deliver after 3 months.
- Manual sell orders remove resources immediately and pay credits after 3 months.
- Auto trades execute at year end and apply immediately.
- Buy orders that overflow capacity are partially delivered and refunded at unit price.

## Purpose Notes
- Nutrition: civilian upkeep and infantry logistics.
- Lumber, steel, copper, alloy: core construction and military hardware.
- Oil, magnet, electricity: advanced industry and weapons systems.
- Glass, polymer, concrete, silicon: mid-to-late game production.
- Uranium: nuclear research and ballistic missiles.
